How can your enemy have feelings?

What Cuphead does great is showing emotion. After a boss beats you, you're shown a quote from the boss taunting you or something of the sort. Even as you're fighting them they might wave their fist in anger or laugh after you get hit. They show emotions which starts to make them seem as more than just a stage to beat and as actual characters.
