Customization, what's that?

Customization allows players to change certain things about a game or its gameplay to suit their tastes. Ex: weapons, armor, stats, enemies, etc.

Cuphead's Great Combat Customization!

Cuphead isn't lacking in combat customization at all. It has multiple ways you can change it up to fit how you play. You can change the main two weapons, your abilities, and even your super attack. While this is resricted to Run & Guns and Boss Fights it is still a nice addition.
