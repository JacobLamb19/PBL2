NPC or Non-Player Character

NPCs are characters in a game that are controlled by AI or artificial intelligence. They usually have dialoge or contribute more to the story/plot. Though they can be enemies they are treated as different things as an enemy will attack a player on sight.

Cuphead's NPCs

Cuphead's world has NPCs or character everywhere. In the overworld where you select your stage you can talk to any character present and they all add something. Some of them even give you things to help you progress in the game.
