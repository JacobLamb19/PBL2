Cuphead has secrets that you can use to alter the game.

There are a lot of secrets that the game can tell you what you need to do to unlock or just give hints about. There are too many to list along with the fact that not all have been found yet.

Just a few

One of the secrets is a turtle in Inkisle III tells you about unlocking black and white mode. Once you beat all the run n' gun levels as a pacifist you get black and white mode. Pacifist means that you beat the levels without firing anything or killing enemies. Another secret is getting a free coin for the shop when you go around the mountain for a secret path. A girl wants to know where the path is by telling her she gives you a coin.
